description: Dana Holleran, known as “Houdana,” is a New Hampshire-based magician with over 40 years of experience. Residing in Manchester, he specializes in comedy stage magic for children and adults, often featuring a live rabbit finale. He also excels in close-up sleight-of-hand magic for one-on-one and strolling performances. Dana’s passion for magic began at Al Cohen’s magic shop in Washington, DC. Over the years, he performed in various venues, from children’s parties to schools, prisons, senior homes, and restaurants. Having performed across Washington, Virginia, West Virginia, and now New Hampshire, he continues to entertain at birthday parties, daycare centers, and special events.
